### **01. Total acumulado de pedidos classificados por data**
```sql
SELECT 
    OrderID,
    CustomerID,
    OrderDate,
    SUM(Freight) OVER (ORDER BY OrderDate) AS TotalAcumulado
FROM Orders;
```

---

### **02. Média móvel dos valores dos pedidos nos últimos 3 pedidos para cada cliente**
```sql

    OrderDate,
    AVG(Freight) OVER (PARTITION BY CustomerID ORDER BY OrderDate ROWS 2 PRECEDING) AS MediaMovel
```

### **03. Produtos com a soma acumulada de vendas por categoria**
```sql

    p.CategoryID,
    p.ProductName,
    SUM(od.Quantity * od.UnitPrice) OVER (PARTITION BY p.CategoryID ORDER BY od.OrderID) AS SomaAcumulada
FROM Products p
```

### **04. Percentual de contribuição dos produtos em vendas dentro da categoria**
```sql
SELECT 
    p.CategoryID,
    p.ProductName,
    SUM(od.Quantity * od.UnitPrice) AS TotalVendas,
    SUM(SUM(od.Quantity * od.UnitPrice)) OVER (PARTITION BY p.CategoryID) AS TotalCategoria,
    SUM(od.Quantity * od.UnitPrice) * 100.0 / SUM(SUM(od.Quantity * od.UnitPrice)) OVER (PARTITION BY p.CategoryID) AS PercentualContribuicao
FROM Products p
JOIN [Order Details] od ON p.ProductID = od.ProductID
GROUP BY p.CategoryID, p.ProductName;
```

### **26. Crescimento percentual do valor de vendas mês a mês**
```sql

    LAG(SUM(Freight)) OVER (ORDER BY YEAR(OrderDate), MONTH(OrderDate)) AS VendasMesAnterior,
    (SUM(Freight) - LAG(SUM(Freight)) OVER (ORDER BY YEAR(OrderDate), MONTH(OrderDate))) * 100.0 /
```

### **35. Média de vendas por estação do ano**
```sql
SELECT 
    ProductID,
    ProductName,
    CASE 
        WHEN MONTH(OrderDate) IN (12, 1, 2) THEN 'Inverno'
        WHEN MONTH(OrderDate) IN (3, 4, 5) THEN 'Primavera'
        WHEN MONTH(OrderDate) IN (6, 7, 8) THEN 'Verão'
        ELSE 'Outono'
    END AS Estacao,
    AVG(od.Quantity * od.UnitPrice) OVER (PARTITION BY ProductID, Estacao) AS MediaEstacao
FROM [Order Details] od
JOIN Orders o ON od.OrderID = o.OrderID
JOIN Products p ON od.ProductID = p.ProductID;
```
